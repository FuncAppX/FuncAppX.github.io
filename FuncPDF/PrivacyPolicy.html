Purchase History (IAP Transaction Data) Purpose: To verify and manage user subscription/purchase rights, confirm the validity period of the service, and provide the purchased service. Device/Usage Information (Collected via Third-Party SDKs)
Including: Coarse Location, Device ID, Product Interaction, Advertising Data, Crash Data, Performance Data, and other Diagnostic Data		 Purpose:

 Some data is sent to our authorized partners (such as Google Mobile Ads/AdMob) for processing and storage.
